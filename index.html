About Me

I'm Larissa Silva

Visual designer and illustrator from Brazil

I have more than 8 years of experience as a freelance graphic designer, focusing on branding and digital communication, in the production of advertisement pieces and marketing for companies and events. In the last few months, I sought to improve myself in Web Design (HTML and CSS) and to train myself in UI/UX Design.

I have a BA in Architecture and Urban Design, and work experience in residential and institutional design projects, as well as in revitalization and urban landscaping.
